# Starting Scenario: Socialist Europe vs Fascist Italy (Alpha)
*Disclaimer: Not yet fully balanced.*
## Overview
This mod provides a fully scripted alternate-history starting scenario for *Victoria 3*. In 1836, Central and Eastern Europe have already undergone successful socialist revolutions. The result is a bipolar continent: a powerful socialist bloc (either the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics or the European Socialist Union) facing a fascist Italian state as its primary ideological and military counterweight.

The mod transforms the default 1836 setup into a Cold-War-style confrontation between radical leftism and fascism, complete with new subject mechanics, dynamic country naming, custom government types, journal entries, and ongoing event chains.

## Features
### Core Gameplay Changes
- **New Subject Type: Constituent Republic**  
  A specialized diplomatic pact with high integration. Subjects retain limited autonomy but contribute significantly to the overlord’s economy and military.

- **Dynamic Country Names**  
  - Soviet-style republics automatically rename to “X SSR” when under Russian (or equivalent) socialist overlordship.  
  - German socialist subjects under North/South German overlords receive “X Volkssektor” naming.

- **Custom Government Type**  
  - **Ducist Regime** for Italy (titles: Duce / Ducessa).  
  - Automatically applied when Italy adopts a fascist corporate-state government (if it works, this government type assignment system is screwing with me since forever).

### Power Blocs & Federations
- **European Socialist Union (ESU)** – optional ideological super-bloc led by North German Federation.  
- **Union of Socialist States** – fallback bloc for USSR-style playthroughs.

### Journal Entries
- **The Bolshevik Menace** (Fascist Italy & other right-wing European nations) – tracks the spread of socialism and urges containment.  
- **Bringing the World Revolution** (Socialist nations) – celebrates continental dominance and pushes for global socialist expansion.

### Events & Modifiers
- **Puppet Management Events**  
  Overlords can pressure socialist puppets to align fully with council-republic laws. Puppets may submit, resist (gaining claims on the overlord), or break free if ideological alignment fails.
- **Strong Ideological Bias Modifiers**  
  Permanent (decaying) state-level modifiers that massively boost communist or fascist political support, ensuring the starting ideological setup remains stable.

### Game Rules (selectable at game start)
- **Create the USSR** – Russia becomes the center of a classic Soviet federation with numerous Constituent Republics (Poland, Ukraine, Baltics, Central Asia, etc.).
- **Create the ESU** – North German Federation leads a pan-European socialist union.
- **Vanilla** – disables all mod content for a standard 1836 start.
