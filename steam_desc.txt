[h1]Starting Scenario: Socialist Europe vs Fascist Italy (Alpha)[/h1]
[i]Disclaimer: Not yet fully balanced.[/i]
[h2]Overview[/h2]
This mod provides a fully scripted alternate-history starting scenario for
[i]Victoria 3[/i]. In 1836, Central and Eastern Europe have already undergone
successful socialist revolutions. The result is a bipolar continent: a powerful
socialist bloc (either the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics or the European
Socialist Union) facing a fascist Italian state as its primary ideological and
military counterweight.

The mod transforms the default 1836 setup into a Cold-War-style confrontation
between radical leftism and fascism, complete with new subject mechanics,
dynamic country naming, custom government types, journal entries, and ongoing
event chains.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[h3]Core Gameplay Changes[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]New Subject Type: Constituent Republic[/b]
[/list]
  A specialized diplomatic pact with high integration. Subjects retain limited
autonomy but contribute significantly to the overlord’s economy and military.

[list]
[*][b]Dynamic Country Names[/b]
  [*]Soviet-style republics automatically rename to “X SSR” when under Russian
(or equivalent) socialist overlordship.
  [*]German socialist subjects under North/South German overlords receive “X
Volkssektor” naming.
[/list]

[list]
[*][b]Custom Government Type[/b]
  [*][b]Ducist Regime[/b] for Italy (titles: Duce / Ducessa).
  [*]Automatically applied when Italy adopts a fascist corporate-state
government (if it works, this government type assignment system is screwing
with me since forever).
[/list]

[h3]Power Blocs & Federations[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]European Socialist Union (ESU)[/b] – optional ideological super-bloc led
by North German Federation.
[*][b]Union of Socialist States[/b] – fallback bloc for USSR-style playthroughs.
[/list]

[h3]Journal Entries[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]The Bolshevik Menace[/b] (Fascist Italy & other right-wing European
nations) – tracks the spread of socialism and urges containment.
[*][b]Bringing the World Revolution[/b] (Socialist nations) – celebrates
continental dominance and pushes for global socialist expansion.
[/list]

[h3]Events & Modifiers[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Puppet Management Events[/b]
[/list]
  Overlords can pressure socialist puppets to align fully with council-republic
laws. Puppets may submit, resist (gaining claims on the overlord), or break
free if ideological alignment fails.
[list]
[*][b]Strong Ideological Bias Modifiers[/b]
[/list]
  Permanent (decaying) state-level modifiers that massively boost communist or
fascist political support, ensuring the starting ideological setup remains
stable.

[h3]Game Rules (selectable at game start)[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Create the USSR[/b] – Russia becomes the center of a classic Soviet
federation with numerous Constituent Republics (Poland, Ukraine, Baltics,
Central Asia, etc.).
[*][b]Create the ESU[/b] – North German Federation leads a pan-European
socialist union.
[*][b]Vanilla[/b] – disables all mod content for a standard 1836 start.
[/list]
